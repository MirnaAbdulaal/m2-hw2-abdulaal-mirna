About Mirna Abdulaal

My interests

My name is Mirna Abdulaal and my interests include communications and finding ways to help governments to effectively communicate their message. I am also really passionate about creative writing, and I believe that journalism is a powerful force in bringing voice to the voiceless. One of my favourite hobbies is horse riding, as I love the connection and emotion I experience with my horse.
